Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate margin?

Enter your Cost and Selling Price in the input fields. Profito instantly calculates your margin percentage, markup percentage, profit amount, and revenue. No "Calculate" button needed â€” results update in real time.

What is the difference between Margin and Markup?

Margin is profit as a percentage of selling price (Profit / Revenue). Markup is profit as a percentage of cost (Profit / Cost). You can switch between both views using the segment toggle at the top.

How does the Tax/VAT feature work?

Toggle the Tax switch to include tax in your calculations. You can set a custom tax rate in the settings. Tax is deducted from revenue, reducing your profit accordingly.

What does the donut chart show?

The donut chart provides a visual breakdown of your selling price into Cost (gray), Profit (green), and Tax (orange) proportions. It updates in real time as you change values.

Does Profito collect my data?

No. Profito does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial data. All calculations happen on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is Profito really free?

Yes. Profito is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.
