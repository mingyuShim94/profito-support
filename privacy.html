Privacy Policy

Profito - Margin Calculator
Last updated: February 19, 2026

Profito does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial data. All calculations happen entirely on your device.

Overview

Profito ("the App") is developed by gguggulab. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Data Collection

Profito does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No financial data is collected or transmitted (costs, prices, margins, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking data is collected
  • No cookies or identifiers are used
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included

How the App Works

All margin and markup calculations are performed entirely on your device. The App:

  • Calculates margin, markup, profit, and revenue locally
  • Stores your preferences (theme, currency, tax rate) on your device only
  • Does not require an internet connection to function
  • Does not send any data to external servers

Data Storage

User preferences (display settings, default tax rate) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is automatically removed when you delete the App. No calculation history is stored.

Third-Party Services

Profito does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
